Need help with Liten?

We've tried to make Liten as easy as possible to use.

Liten Quickstart:

  1. 1. Click “Find Duplicates” and select the folder or library you want to search.
  2. 2. Click “OK” and it will quickly and safely identify duplicate files by comparing possible matches against a sha 256 hash.
  3. 3. You can then right click on matches and explore them in the finder, do additional searches in spotlight, or send a duplicate to the trash.

Tip: Sort by the size column to see the largest files on top.

A new feature is the support for doing multiple deletion. This works by selecting multiple files, and then control or right clicking "move to trash". A confirmation dialog will ask you if you are sure you want to move these to the trash. It is highly recommended that you verify the contents of your trash before you empty it after a multiple delete.

While Liten does perform the safest possible comparison algorithm, you should be extremely cautious when deleting files. One item in particular to watch out for is deleting important system files, or application files. Only delete an item when you are very sure of why it is a duplicate.
